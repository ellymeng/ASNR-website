Contribute

One of the main goals of this repository is to further enhance the study of animal social networks. If you happen to come across any interesting data on animal networks that has been published but has not yet been included in this repository, please feel free to contact us! Fill out the form below and include a brief description of the data, the format of the dataset, and where/how to download it. After you submit the form, you will be redirected to a Google form where you can upload the dataset as an attachment. We will do our best to make it consistent with our format and add it to the repository.
